How to get the best results: On the HRN entries page, scroll past the navigation and select from the first race header down to the last horse in the final race. The parser looks for patterns like Churchill Downs Race # 1, horse numbers, odds like 8/1 or 8-1, and post times like 12:45 PM. The more complete the paste, the better the result.
